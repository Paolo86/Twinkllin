    We strongly believe in the power of art

    Twinkllin was founded in 2018 by talented artist Wen Lin Liang. After her degree in art, she decided to venture in the world of jewelery to take her unique artistic vision to this new field. The brand is set to inspire others to believe in their vision of art and pursue the carrer they desire.

    Our products

    Crafting love, Twinkllin is a brand artisan of love and a weaver of dreams – turning raw silver into beautiful visions of love; interpreting every emotion into meaningful designs with a story behind each jewel. Inspired by surreal, fantasy worlds and mythical creatures, Twinkllin's jewellery style evokes true feelings of wonder and a sense of diiscovery and adventure. Keeping true to her values and a respect for the basics, she strikes a harmony in her designs between prestige and authenticity. Each creation is the birth of a story unique to one.

    Where are we?

    We always welcome customers to our laboratory located in Perth, WA. We are happy to show you around so come over for a coffee when you can.
